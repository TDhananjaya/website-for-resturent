About us...


Japanese food has long been considered as one of the healthiest cuisines in the world is gradually growing in Sri Lanka. Due to its rarity and high price it became popular only among the affluent clientele.

This we saw as a opportunity for a new brand to enter in to the market and make Japanese food more affordable and easily accessible via door to door delivery. Thus Tanoshi was born in 2015, opening doors to Japanese food lovers and especially the adventurous customers who like to experience new cuisines.

Tanoshi is currently the only Japanese restaurant in Kurunegala to offer delivery services. At Tanoshi, which means “Enjoyable” in Japanese we believe in offering our customers the very best quality at reasonable prices.
